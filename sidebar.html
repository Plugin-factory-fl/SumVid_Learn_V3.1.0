About Eureka AI

Eureka AI is a powerful Chrome extension that helps learners and educators enhance their learning experience. Get AI-powered summaries, flashcards, quizzes, and interactive chat for any YouTube video or webpage content.

Getting Started

For YouTube Videos:

  1. Open any YouTube video
  2. Click the Eureka AI icon in your browser toolbar
  3. Use Summarize, Flashcards, or Test features to enhance your learning

For Webpages:

  1. Navigate to any webpage
  2. Click the Eureka AI icon in your browser toolbar
  3. Chat with AI about the content or generate summaries

Summarize this content

Generate AI-powered summaries of videos, webpages, or PDFs. Get concise, easy-to-understand summaries that help you learn faster. This feature costs 1 use per summary.

Flashcard Maker

Create interactive flashcards from any content to help you memorize key concepts. Study smarter with AI-generated flashcards tailored to your learning material. This feature costs 1 use per flashcard set.

Test Your Knowledge

Test your understanding with AI-generated quizzes. Get instant feedback on your knowledge and identify areas that need more study. This feature costs 1 use per quiz.

Notes

Organize your learning with notes. Save important information, create folders, and keep all your study materials in one place. Notes are free to use and help you stay organized.
